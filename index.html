At The creative guys , we create meaningful and engaging videos for our clients. The entire video content is created and customized to cater to our clients’ needs. Whether you need a marketing video to improve your brand visibility or a product demo to market your services, our team excels in producing premium quality videos. We help you create any type of video from scratch. From brainstorming different ideas to the final delivery, our process involves a series of meticulous animation and production stages. Here is a quick summary of our process: Understanding your business and marketing objectives Story and script development Storyboard Voiceover Illustration Animation Delivery If you want more information about our process, we’ve explained it in detail on our video production process page. If that’s not enough, we’ll be happy to explain it over a call.

You’ll get unlimited revisions for script development. We want to make sure your story is well crafted and is the best it can be before moving forward. All other stages; storyboard, voiceover, illustration, and animation come with two revisions each, to make sure we polish each to be the best it can be.

A typical explainer video should be at least 60 seconds, and 90 to 120 seconds for more complex subjects. Because each client and topic is different, the actual length will depend on several variables.

You can reach us using our contact form, and we’ll provide a free consultation and gather details of your project. We’ll provide a quick quote for your consideration, and once we agree on pricing and delivery terms, we’ll draft an agreement to proceed.
